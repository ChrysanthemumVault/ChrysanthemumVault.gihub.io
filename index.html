ETHOS

A Cabinet of Wonders—built for the present tense.

Chrysanthemum Vault is a studio and a ledger. Paintings, relics, and curios are fabricated with archival attention and meant to be handled, worn, or displayed in living spaces—not vitrines alone. Works carry the quiet gravity of discovered artifacts while remaining decisively contemporary.

Commissions are welcomed when they serve the piece. Teaching and workshops are offered in drawing, surface, and small-object construction. Inquiries are answered personally.
