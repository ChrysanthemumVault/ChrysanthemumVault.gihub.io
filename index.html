About

The Vault

The Chrysanthemum Vault is a studio‑gallery and cabinet of wonders. Here, paintings, relics, and artifacts share space with handmade jewelry, curiosities, and rare objects. Each piece is crafted or chosen with precision, presented as if discovered in a reliquary or naturalist’s archive. The Vault is both a gallery and a workshop — a place where objects are made, displayed, and taught with reverence for their story.
